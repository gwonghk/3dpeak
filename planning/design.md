# 3dpeak Design Redesign — 2026-04-16

## Target Audience
- Gift-shoppers
- Hobbyists
- **Vibe:** approachable & fun

---

## Color Palette

- **Primary:** Coral/Peach — `#FF6B6B` or `#FF9F89` (warm, friendly, gift-worthy)
- **Secondary:** Mint/Teal — `#06D6A0` or `#64DFDF` (playful, not childish)
- **Background:** Warm cream — `#FFF8F0` (not cold white/gray)
- **Text:** Warm charcoal (not pure black)

## Typography
- Display font (headings): Fredoka, Quicksand, or Sora — rounded, friendly
- Body: Clean sans-serif

## Visual Personality
- Organic blob shapes as decorative backgrounds
- Soft drop shadows (not harsh)
- Large border-radius everywhere (pill buttons, rounded cards)
- Subtle texture/grain overlay
- Tactile feel throughout

## Product Cards
- Lift + slight rotate on hover (feels like picking something up)
- Category tags with pastel backgrounds
- Friendly "Inquire" button

## Interactions
- Bouncy micro-animations — buttons that squish on click
- Cards that wiggle on hover
- Smooth scroll reveals

## Hero Section Ideas
- Split layout: text left, bold product photo right
- Or: warm illustrated bg with floating 3D print objects
- Conversational copy (drop corporate tone)

## Component Inspiration
- 21st.dev community components (requires JS for live preview)
- Categories to explore: Heros, Features, Buttons, Cards, Testimonials
